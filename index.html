Neptune

Neptune is the only planet of our system that is invisible to the unaid eye. Thus, it was also the first planet to be discovered by mathematical predictions. Neptune is referred to as an ice giant, and holds the most powerful wind speeds of the Solar System.

Scientific soundness

The aim of this project is to provide elegant information about the Solar System. However, given how huge and complex it is, the 3D modelling could not be scientifically correct. For the sake of simplicity and esthetism, the relative sizes of the planets and sun have been reduced, the orbits are simplified, the distances between the planets are shortened, and their movements are faster. The majority of the planets' textures are inspired from real footage, but Neptune and Uranus are artistic views.
